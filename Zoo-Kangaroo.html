Kangaroo

The Kangaroo is a marsupial who is indigenous to Australia and New Guinea. The most common Kangaroo is Red Kangaroo, which is also the largest of the species.

Some Of it's characteristics are:

  • Short hair and powerful hind legs
  • Excellent hearing and keen eyesight
  • A long tail to balance themselves with
  • Carry their young, called joeys, in pouches on their torso
